# Composting Toilet Construction & Use

## Overview

Simple bucket: 5-gal bucket, toilet seat, sawdust cover. Two-bin: fill one while other composts, rotate 6-12mo. Sawdust/cover: wood shavings, leaves, grass - absorbs moisture, reduces odor. C:N ratio 30:1. Pathogen destruction: time 1+yr, temp 130°F+ hot composting or passive 2+yrs. Urine diversion: reduces odor, dilute 1:10 for fertilizer. Finished compost: non-food plants first year. Emergency: cat hole 6-8" deep, 200ft from water.

⚠️ **SAFETY WARNING:** This is a critical survival entry. Follow protocols carefully. Seek professional medical help when available.
