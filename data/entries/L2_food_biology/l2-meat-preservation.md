## Overview
Preservation workflow balancing dehydration, smoke exposure, and microbial risk.

## Step-by-step
1. Trim fat and slice uniformly for drying.
2. Maintain controlled low heat and airflow.
3. Use clean racks and protect from insects.
4. Store in dry containers and monitor spoilage indicators.

## Warnings
- Improper temperatures can promote pathogen growth.
- Botulism risk exists in low-oxygen storage.
