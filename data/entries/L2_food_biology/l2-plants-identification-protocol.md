## Overview
Conservative identification protocol requiring multiple confirming features and lookalike exclusion.

## Step-by-step
1. Determine region and season before candidate list.
2. Verify morphology across leaf/stem/flower/fruit traits.
3. Cross-check toxic lookalikes in two independent references.
4. Use safe preparation method from authoritative guide.

## Warnings
- Never consume a plant with uncertain identification.
- Some toxins persist after cooking.
