## Overview
Durable non-metal and mixed joinery patterns for repairs and simple structures.

## Step-by-step
1. Choose joint type based on load direction.
2. Cut clean mating surfaces.
3. Use pegs/lashings where metal fasteners unavailable.
4. Test load progressively before use.

## Warnings
- Improper joint orientation can fail catastrophically.
- Moisture cycling weakens loose fits.
