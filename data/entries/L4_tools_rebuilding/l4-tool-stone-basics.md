## Overview
Core stone reduction concepts for simple cutting/scraping tools.

## Step-by-step
1. Select cryptocrystalline or fine-grain stone where available.
2. Prepare platform angle and strike direction.
3. Remove flakes progressively to shape edge.
4. Retouch edge for intended task.

## Warnings
- Eye and hand protection required.
- Sharp flakes cause severe lacerations.
