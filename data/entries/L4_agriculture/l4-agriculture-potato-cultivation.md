# Potato Cultivation & Storage

## Overview

Highest calories/acre. Planting: seed potatoes with eyes, 4" deep, 12" apart. Hilling: mound soil as grow (prevents green/toxic). Varieties: early 70-90d, mid 90-110d, late 110-140d. Water: 1-2" /week. Harvest: when vines die, cure in shade 1-2wks. Storage: root cellar 38-40°F, high humidity, dark, check rot weekly. Yield: 10lb seed → 100-200lb harvest.

⚠️ **SAFETY WARNING:** This is a critical survival entry. Follow protocols carefully. Seek professional medical help when available.
