## Overview
Operational governance concepts for legitimacy, accountability, and conflict reduction.

## Step-by-step
1. Define clear roles and limited authority.
2. Use transparent rule-making and records.
3. Create fair dispute-resolution process.
4. Review outcomes and adapt rules periodically.

## Warnings
- Power concentration without accountability increases abuse risk.
- Opaque decisions degrade trust and compliance.
