## Overview
Boiling is an accessible method for microbiological risk reduction in emergency water treatment.

## Step-by-step
1. Pre-filter turbid water using cloth or settling.
2. Bring water to rolling boil.
3. Cool in covered sanitized container.
4. Prevent post-treatment contamination during storage.

## Warnings
- Boiling does not remove heavy metals or industrial chemicals.
- Storage container hygiene is critical.
