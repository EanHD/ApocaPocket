## Overview
Immediate burn response emphasizing cooling, contamination control, and escalation triggers.

## Step-by-step
1. Remove from heat source and stop burning process.
2. Cool with clean running water; avoid ice.
3. Cover with clean non-adherent dressing.
4. Monitor for airway involvement and shock.

## Warnings
- Chemical/electrical burns need specialized management.
- Large burns require urgent evacuation.
