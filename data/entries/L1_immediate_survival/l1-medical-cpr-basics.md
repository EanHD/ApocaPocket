## Overview
Core CPR sequence for lay responders based on Red Cross and WHO frameworks.

## Step-by-step
1. Confirm scene safety and check responsiveness.
2. Call emergency services and request AED.
3. Begin chest compressions with correct depth/rate.
4. Use AED as soon as available and follow prompts.

## Warnings
- CPR technique varies by patient age and training level.
- Do not delay emergency services activation.
