## Overview
Mechanical filtration for particulates and some pathogens, with treatment-chain integration.

## Step-by-step
1. Assess source quality and turbidity.
2. Use rated filter with known pore size and maintenance schedule.
3. Follow with disinfection when required.
4. Track filter lifespan and clean per manufacturer guidance.

## Warnings
- Most field filters do not remove dissolved chemicals.
- Virus removal often requires additional disinfection.
