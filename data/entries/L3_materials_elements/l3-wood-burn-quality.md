## Overview
Fuel selection by moisture, density, and desired heat curve.

## Step-by-step
1. Separate tinder/kindling/fuel logs by diameter.
2. Prefer seasoned wood for stable combustion.
3. Blend fast and slow-burning species.
4. Ventilate combustion area to reduce smoke/CO.

## Warnings
- Green wood increases smoke and creosote.
- Indoor combustion without ventilation is dangerous.
