# Vehicle Winter Survival

## Overview

Stay vs go: stay if blizzard/night/<1mi to help. Run engine 10min/hr, check exhaust clear. CO poisoning: crack window 1". Insulation: floor mats on roof, newspaper in doors. Snow melting for water. Signal: bright cloth on antenna. Winter kit: blankets, candles, food, water.
