# Epilepsy & Seizure Management

## Overview

Seizure first aid: protect head, time duration, recovery position. DON'T restrain or put objects in mouth. Status epilepticus (>5min)=emergency. Medications: phenytoin, carbamazepine, valproic acid. Triggers: sleep deprivation, lights, stress. Post-ictal confusion normal.

⚠️ **SAFETY WARNING:** This is a critical survival entry. Follow protocols carefully. Seek professional medical help when available.
