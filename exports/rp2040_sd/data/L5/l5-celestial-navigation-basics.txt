[SUMMARY]
Foundational celestial fixes using sun/stars and time references.

[STEPS]
1. Establish accurate time reference.
2. Measure altitude of known celestial body.
3. Apply reduction tables or precomputed methods.
4. Cross-check with dead reckoning and terrain cues.

[WARNINGS]
! Time errors create major positional errors.
! Cloud cover and refraction limit practical use.
