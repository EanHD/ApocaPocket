[SUMMARY]
Basic carcass handling to reduce contamination and spoilage risk.

[STEPS]
1. Use clean tools and avoid GI tract puncture.
2. Remove viscera promptly and cool carcass.
3. Isolate suspect tissue and signs of disease.
4. Store and transport meat at safe temperatures.

[WARNINGS]
! Wild game can carry pathogens and parasites.
! Discard meat with abnormal odor/discoloration.
