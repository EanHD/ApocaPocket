[SUMMARY]
Core knots for attaching hooks, joining lines, and creating loops for fishing applications.

[STEPS]
1. Improved clinch knot: primary hook-to-line attachment. 5 wraps through eye, back through loop.
2. Palomar knot: strongest simple knot for hooks/swivels. Double line through eye, overhand knot, pass hook through loop.
3. Surgeon's knot: join two lines of different diameter. Overlap ends, double overhand, pull tight.
4. Loop knot: non-slip mono loop for lure movement. Overhand knot in line, pass through eye, wrap 5x, back through overhand.

[WARNINGS]
! Wet knots before tightening to prevent heat damage to line.
! Test every knot before use.
