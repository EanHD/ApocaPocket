[SUMMARY]
Mushroom identification workflow emphasizing exclusion of deadly lookalikes.

[STEPS]
1. Photograph and document habitat/substrate.
2. Take spore print and record color/time.
3. Use cap, gill, stem, bruising traits with key references.
4. Reject specimen if any trait conflicts or remains unknown.

[WARNINGS]
! Mushroom poisoning can be fatal with delayed symptoms.
! Do not rely on folklore tests for edibility.
