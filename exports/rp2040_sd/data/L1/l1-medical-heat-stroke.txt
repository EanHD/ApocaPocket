[SUMMARY]
Differentiation and immediate treatment priorities for heat illness.

[STEPS]
1. Move to shade and remove excess clothing.
2. Actively cool using water and airflow.
3. Hydrate if conscious and not vomiting.
4. Escalate rapidly if altered mental status.

[WARNINGS]
! Heat stroke is life-threatening and time-critical.
! Do not give oral fluids to unconscious patients.
