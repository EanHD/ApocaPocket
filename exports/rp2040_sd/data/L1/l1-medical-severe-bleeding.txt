[SUMMARY]
Initial hemorrhage control prioritizing direct pressure and appropriate tourniquet use.

[STEPS]
1. Apply firm direct pressure with clean cloth/dressing.
2. If life-threatening extremity bleed continues, apply tourniquet proximal to wound.
3. Record time of tourniquet application.
4. Monitor for shock and expedite evacuation.

[WARNINGS]
! Improper tourniquet placement can worsen injury.
! Internal bleeding requires urgent advanced care.
