[SUMMARY]
Comparative ignition options under varying moisture and wind conditions.

[STEPS]
1. Prepare tinder and kindling before ignition attempt.
2. Start with most reliable method available (spark/flame source).
3. Use progressively larger fuel sizes to build coal bed.
4. Maintain safe clearance and extinguish completely.

[WARNINGS]
! Wildfire risk increases with wind and dry fuels.
! Use ventilation and carbon monoxide controls in shelters.
