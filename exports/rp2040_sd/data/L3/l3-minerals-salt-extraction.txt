[SUMMARY]
Methods for locating and extracting salt from natural deposits, seawater, and brine springs.

[STEPS]
1. Coastal: collect seawater in shallow pans, allow solar evaporation over days.
2. Inland: locate salt licks (areas where animals gather), brine springs, or surface salt deposits.
3. Boil brine in wide, shallow containers to accelerate evaporation.
4. Scrape crystallized salt and re-dissolve in clean water to purify, then re-evaporate.
5. Plant indicator: salt-tolerant plants (glasswort, sea lavender) indicate saline soils.
