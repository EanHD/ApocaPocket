## Quality Standards Applied

### Measurements & Specifications
- **Temperature ranges:** Specific values with units (°C, °F where relevant)
- **Dimensions:** Metric with precision (mm for tools, cm for spacing, m for structures)
- **Weight/Mass:** kg for large, g for small, with yield percentages
- **Chemical formulas:** Molecular formulas where applicable (KOH, Al2Si2O5(OH)4, etc.)
- **Time specifications:** Hours/days/weeks with ranges (2-4 hours, 6-8 weeks)
- **Ratios:** Precise proportions (3:1, 50/50, 10-30% by volume)

### Safety Protocols
- **PPE specifications:** Exact equipment types (N95 vs P100, 15+ oz leather gloves)
- **Exposure limits:** OSHA standards where applicable (2 mg/m³ lye dust)
- **First aid:** Immediate treatment steps (15-20 min water flush, when to seek medical care)
- **Distance requirements:** Safe zones (3+ meters for stone knapping, 30+ meters buck pens)
- **Hazard warnings:** Specific risks with consequences (silicosis, chemical burns, collapse)

### Technical Details
- **Material properties:** Hardness (Mohs scale), tensile strength (MPa), melting points
- **Efficiency data:** Yields per hour/day, fuel consumption, conversion ratios
- **Load calculations:** Forces, stresses, safety factors, deflection limits
- **Testing procedures:** Step-by-step with pass/fail criteria
- **Troubleshooting:** Common problems with specific solutions
